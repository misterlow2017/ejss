Block On An Incline: Coefficient of Friction

Find the static coefficient of friction for the Block Sliding On An Incline. This simulation uses the accelerometer on your mobile device to read the direction of the gravitation field g. You may need to lock the orientation of your screen to maintain a fixed view as you tilt your device. For use on computers without an accelerometer or for classroom use, the "No sensor" mode simulates a tilted device in the view.

Note: Because continuously reading the accelerometer may drain the battery on a mobile device, you must run the simulation to read the gravitational field g. The simulation will automatically pause after 4 minutes to conserve battery power.
